Arrencant el dia

First edition of the 112 Revelats photographic challenge

August 8, 2025 — December 22, 2025

Download "Starting the day" (PDF)

Arrencant el dia is the first collective photobook from 112 Revelats. A visual exploration of the moment the day begins, captured through diverse and personal perspectives.

The challenge

The challenge was to capture the moment the day begins. Each participant freely interpreted this moment: from the first light coming through the window to the morning bustle of the street, through twilight landscapes and intimate portraits of dawn.

The submissions were extraordinarily diverse, demonstrating the richness of perspectives that a single theme can generate. The selection process was intense, and choosing the final images was very difficult.

The photobook

The book features 24 images selected from over 80 submissions. Each author participates with 3 photographs, forming a collective narrative that moves from darkness to full light, in a visual journey through different moments of the morning.

Acknowledgements

We deeply thank all the people who participated in this first edition. Both the selected authors and those who could not be in the final book: your work and your trust make 112 Revelats possible.
