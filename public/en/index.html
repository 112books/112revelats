What is 112 Revelats?

112 Revelats is a collective editorial project that promotes photobooks with emerging and established authors, fostering contemporary photography and collaborative work between creators.

Each edition proposes a thematic challenge. Participants contribute their unique perspective and together build a one-of-a-kind photobook, published digitally and, when sponsorship allows, in print as well.

Whether you are an emerging photographer or an established artist, 112 Revelats is a space to share, create, and publish collectively.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is 112 Revelats?

A collective editorial project that promotes photobooks with emerging and established authors.

How can I participate?

Every six months we open a photo challenge. Send us your email and we will let you know when the next call opens.

Do I have to pay to participate?

No. Participating in the challenge is free. The digital photobook is published at no cost. For a print edition, we seek collective sponsorship.

What happens to my photos?

Selected photos become part of the collective photobook, with credit for each author. You retain all rights to your images.

How can I contribute as a patron?

You can make a donation for the print edition of the photobook, or sponsor an emerging author. Fill in the collaboration form and we will explain the options.
