---
title: "112 Revelats"
description: "Collective photobook project — 112Books"
---

112 Revelats is a collective editorial project that promotes photobooks with emerging and established authors, fostering contemporary photography and collaborative work between creators.

Each edition proposes a thematic challenge. Participants contribute their unique perspective and together build a one-of-a-kind photobook, published digitally and, when sponsorship allows, in print as well.

Whether you are an emerging photographer or an established artist, 112 Revelats is a space to share, create, and publish collectively.
