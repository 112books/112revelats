At **112books.eu** we believe that publishing is an act of cultural resistance. That's why we promote **112 Revelats**, a collective project that gives voice to emerging and established photographers through the creation of photobooks around shared visual challenges.

Bringing a book to life —with dignity and quality— requires resources. That's why we open the door to sponsors, patrons and collaborators who want to join the project and make it possible.

---

## How can you collaborate?

### Direct financial contribution

To contribute to the production and edition of the printed book: paper, printing, transport, shipping... And, above all, to compensate the professionals involved —photographers, designers, editors, writers, layout artists—. At 112books.eu we defend a culture that values and rewards creative work.

### In-kind donation

Photographic material, books on photography, photobooks, software applications, merchandise or other useful products for photographers. If you are part of a company in the sector or have access to materials of interest, this is a direct and meaningful way to offer support.

### Logistical and communication support

Offering spaces to present the project, exhibit the works or host gatherings. Also media channels such as magazines, blogs, podcasts or specialised outlets. Any help that amplifies the project's voice is welcome.

---

## What does the project offer in return?

- **Recognition and visibility**: Featured presence on the website and in the credits page of each book. Mention in promotional materials, public presentations and social media. Possibility to include your logo in printed editions.
- **Active participation in visual culture**: Being part of a project that promotes collective creation, critical thinking and independent publishing. Connection with a network of emerging and established visual creators.
- **Tax benefits**: Financial contributions may be considered tax-deductible expenses under corporate tax (or income tax for freelancers), provided there is a sponsorship or advertising collaboration relationship.

## Non-financial collaborations

At 112 Revelats we accept non-financial collaborations that help make the project possible:

- **Outreach and communication**: advertising space, media coverage, social media collaborations.
- **Products**: photographic material, paper, ink, packaging, books, merchandise.
- **Services**: printing, framing, transport, exhibition setup.
- **Spaces**: exhibition rooms, venues for presentations, studios or sets.
- **Material resources**: technical equipment, exhibition furniture, lighting.

In-kind collaborations receive the same rewards as financial sponsors of equivalent value. The contribution's value is estimated at approximate market price and assigned to the corresponding tier.
